Beauty of Nature

  • Isuru Dinuranga
  • TRAVEL

What is the very first thing that comes into your mind when you hear the word “Travel”? Perhaps your mind won’t give out just one but many.

I too have different points of view on that matter. Some people travel for fun since it’s their hobby while some travel to explore the world. They embrace the new things they see, hear and sense everyday. Some people find travelling as a way of calming their monotonous, busy life.

However, now I am going to share a travelling experience of my own. I was able to visit the village called Daraniyagala which happens to be my father's birthplace. I went there during the new year vacation with my family members. It was a village full of mountain ranges, rivers and waterfalls which reminded me of the first time I saw the beauty of this place. Mere view of this place will heal a depressed mind effortlessly.

A special place which we visited during this trip was the Rakkanawa waterfall. It is a tiny waterfall which falls down through a huge range of rocks while freshening up the people nearby by splashing small droplets of water on them. This place is capable of attracting more and more people every single day by its unique beauty. The house where we spent the night was the place where my father was born. It was situated on a mountain. We can see a lot of mountains, rivers, trees and Sri Pada as well if we look down from there. There is a saying, “Country wind is food for the soul” which I think is so true since these views help to open ourselves up to the nature. Though it is not the first time I visited Daraniyagala this is the first time that I was able to open myself up to the nature and feel this amazing feeling which was rich with freedom, beauty, calmness and many more.

This is my favourite place on Earth now because when I go there I feel this new balanced blend of emotions which now I’m irresistibly in love with. I will never forget these few days for the rest of my life.

This is why I thought of sharing this with you and I think it has been an inspiration for you too to embrace our Mother Nature. Nature will be your ultimate satisfaction if you allow it to be. So finally, I invite all of you to go feel the love and freedom of mother nature while protecting her. Thank you so much for reading my blog and let’s meet again with another interesting blog entry.
